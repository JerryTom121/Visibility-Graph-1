Historic inflation Luxembourg - CPI inflation
Historic inflation Luxembourg (CPI) - This page features an overview of the historic Luxembourg inflation: CPI Luxembourg. The inflation rate is based upon the consumer price index (CPI). Two overviews are being presented:
  • the annual inflation by year for Luxembourg - comparing the december CPI to the december CPI of the year before and
  • the average inflation by year for Luxembourg - the average of 12 monthly inflation rates of a calendar year
In case you click on CPI Luxembourg plus year in the inflation table, you will be directed to a page showing the historic inflation rates of that specific year in Luxembourg. Using the tabs you can switch between the historic CPI inflation overview and the historic HICP inflation overview. When you are interested in the current inflation in Luxembourg (CPI), click here. In case you are interested in an overview of the current inflation by country (CPI), click here.
